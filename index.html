Hello!

I'm currently an undergraduate at Saint Leo University, majoring in psychololgy. I am a researcher with a wide variety of interests, including the psychology of weather and climate, decision-making, autism, empathy, systemizing, sex differences, theory of mind, mental health, self-concept, stigma, interpersonal relationships, pro-social behavior, morality, color vision, and tropical cyclones.

Upon graduating with my B.A., I intend to pursue a PhD in social psychology.

In my spare time, I'm a semi-pro photographer. My interest in photography grew from an extreme childhood interest in weather. Correspondingly, my photographs are focused around weather-, nature-, land- and city- scapes, but are ultimately varied in nature. Check out my photos!
